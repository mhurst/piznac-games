How to Play — 2048

Objective

Slide tiles to combine matching numbers. Reach the 2048 tile to win — but you can keep going!

How Tiles Merge

When two tiles of the same number touch in the direction you slide, they merge into one with their combined value. 2+2 = 4, 4+4 = 8, and so on.

Scoring

Each merge adds the new tile's value to your score. Every move spawns a new 2 (or sometimes a 4). Game ends when the board fills and no merges are possible.

Controls

Arrow keys or W A S D to slide tiles. All tiles move as far as they can in that direction.
