How to Play — Text Twist

Objective

Find a 7-letter word before time runs out to clear the round and advance. Keep going as long as you can.

Rounds

Each round gives you 2:30. Shorter words score points too and carry over round-to-round. Timer resets each new round.

Scoring

3 = 1 pt  •  4 = 2  •  5 = 3  •  6 = 4  •  7 = 5 + 3 pt bonus
